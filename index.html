MY PHISOLOPHY

Integrating Intelligent Agents with Scalable Data Architecture

I focus on building solutions that combine artificial intelligence with robust, enterprise-grade database design. Whether deploying local fine-tuned LLMs for specialized search tasks or orchestrating multi-agent chains with LangGraph, my goal is to deliver performant systems that solve real operational bottlenecks.

On the database side, I specialize in moving legacy systems (Oracle, MySQL) to modern PostgreSQL environments. I tackle hard problems like zero-downtime synchronization using Postgres Foreign Data Wrappers (FDW), connection pooling via PgBouncer, and custom scheduling with pgAgent to keep transactional pipelines fluid.
